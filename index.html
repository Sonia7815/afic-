    About Us

    As one of the country's finest hospitals, the Armed Forces Institute of Cardiology and National Institute of Heart Diseases (AFIC-NIHD) has a proud heritage of serving the country for more than 35 years. This state-of-the-art tertiary care cardiac center with 404 beds is located in the heart of Rawalpindi, the twin city of the federal capital, Islamabad. AFIC-NIHD has the honor to be serving a large population of the upper Punjab, the federal capital, and Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province, Azad Kashmir. and referred cases from the Armed Forces Hospitals all over the country. With the presence of highly qualified specialists and progressive & active residency training programs in cardiology, cardiac surgery, and cardiac anesthesia, AFIC-NIHD has gained the status of a model hospital in the country. AFIC-NIHD is the pioneer in the use of the Impella device during angioplasty, performing Transcatheter aortic valve implantation, and left atrial appendage occlusions, transcatheter aortic aneurysm repair. Another upcoming cutting edge technology is laser lead extraction. Through its teaching mission, AFIC-NIHD helps to train not only physicians, but also nurses and a variety of other healthcare professionals in various disciplines, through continuous medical education (CME) and continuous professional development (CPD), in order to maintain the quality of care offered to the present generation and those to come. The hospital is noted for its devotion to patients, dedication to its professional goals, its motivation to the achievement of excellence, and a very caring and collaborative spirit. AFIC-NIHD is renowned for its high-tech, high-touch compassionate care. Accredited by Pakistan Medical & Dental Council (PMDC) and College of Physicians & Surgeons Pakistan (CPSP), AFIC-NIHD is also proud to be Pakistan Army's first hospital facility to be ISO 9001:2000 certified.

    Commdant Message

    With a legacy spanning over 46 years, the Armed Forces Institute of Cardiology and National Institute of Heart Diseases (AFIC & NIHD) stands as one of the nation's premier healthcare facilities. Situated in the heart of Rawalpindi, adjacent to the federal capital, Islamabad, this cutting-edge tertiary care cardiac center boasts 400 beds. AFIC & NIHD takes pride in its service to a diverse populace, including the upper Punjab, federal capital, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province, Gilgit-Baltistan, Azad Kashmir and referrals from Armed Forces Hospitals nationwide. Benefiting from a cadre of extensively trained specialists and dynamic residency training initiatives in cardiology, interventional cardiology, cardiac surgery, and cardiac anesthesia, AFIC&NIHD has ascended to the pinnacle of healthcare excellence within the nation. Through a strategic partnership with the International Children Heart Foundation (ICHF) USA, the hospital has made remarkable strides in advancing its capabilities in surgical procedures and intensive care for complex congenital heart conditions in pediatric patients. By virtue of its educational endeavors, AFIC & NIHD contributes to the training of not just physicians, but also nurses and a diverse array of healthcare professionals across multiple disciplines. This also includes hosting international conferences on cardiology, cardiac surgery, electrophysiology, and pediatric cardiology. Through ongoing medical education (CME) and continuous professional development (CPD) initiatives, the institution ensures the perpetuation of high-quality care for both current and future generations. Presently, in partnership with AFIC, the federal government is actively engaged in constructing NEPCARD (National institute of Heart Diseases Center of Excellence for Preventive cardiovascular research and development). This facility will function as the national hub for collaborative research, data analysis, and the eventual formulation of guidelines. These guidelines will shape public policies aimed at benefitting the masses. The hospital is recognized for its unwavering commitment to patient welfare, its steadfast pursuit of professional objectives, its drive for excellence, and a deeply compassionate and cooperative ethos. AFIC & NIHD is celebrated for delivering compassionate care that seamlessly integrates cutting-edge technology with a human touch. Endorsed by the Pakistan Medical & Dental Council (PMDC) and the College of Physicians & Surgeons Pakistan (CPSP), AFIC & NIHD takes pride in being the inaugural hospital within the Pakistan Army to achieve ISO 9001:2000 certification. To delve deeper into our hospital's offerings, I extend a warm invitation to peruse our new website and visit it frequently for the latest updates.
